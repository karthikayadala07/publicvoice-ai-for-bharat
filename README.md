## 🧠 About

**PublicVoice AI** is a next-generation civic engagement platform that helps citizens report issues, explore government schemes, and receive intelligent assistance — all in one place.

From road problems to welfare schemes, everything is simplified with AI-driven guidance and multilingual support.

> Built to bridge the gap between citizens and governance.

## ✨ Features

### 🧾 Smart Complaint System

* Raise complaints easily
* Categorize issues (roads, water, electricity, etc.)
* AI-assisted complaint suggestions

### 🤖 AI Assistant (Sahayak)

* Chat-based help for users
* Guides on schemes & complaints
* 24/7 smart assistance

### 🎯 Scheme Finder

* Discover government schemes
* Filter by age, income, category, occupation
* Step-by-step application guidance

### 🎓 Career Guidance

* Explore career paths
* AI-based career suggestions
* After 10th, 12th, graduation support

### 🌍 Multi-Language Support

* English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil & more
* Voice + text interaction

### 🎤 Voice Interaction

* Speak your queries
* AI understands and responds

## ⚙️ How It Works

1️⃣ Enter your details (age, income, category)
2️⃣ Get personalized scheme recommendations
3️⃣ Raise complaints or ask AI assistant
4️⃣ Follow guided steps to apply or resolve issues
